Small Hotel – new 3star business hotel in Vinnytsya.

About the hotel

  • Small Hotel – new 3star business hotel in Vinnytsya.
    We have 28 comfort rooms, fully fitted in correspondence to all modern European standards. Reception and room service are available 24/7.
    Breakfast is included in room cost and served AM 8:00-11:00.
    Check-in – 14:00, Check-out – 12:00.

  • Small Hotel is based on 2floors of S)mall TC in Vyshenka district, that has perfect transport roots, reastaurants and cafes, shops and leisure centers are within walking distance.
    Distance to the city center – 5 km, to “Roshen” multimedia fountain – 6,4 km, railway station – 8 km, West bus station- 1,8 km.

  • Each hotel room has bed with orthopedic mattress, individual bathroom with bath cabin, free WIFI, SMART TV, mini bar and safe.
    Free parking lots are available for our guests with vehicles. One of the rooms is suitable for people with additional needs and is equipped with essential facilities.
